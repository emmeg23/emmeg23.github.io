Our Favorite Wedding for Inspo





One of our favorite weddings from 2022 was Carly Cucco's "Heaven on Earth" Wedding on the Amalfi Coast. This beautiful bride planned a 3 day paradise on the Amalfi coast for all of her guests. Every single detail was thought out and planned to the last second. I never knew something could be so perfect. If you are looking for an Italian themed wedding this is the one for you. And whenyou thought nothing could get any better, it did, she wore custom Oscar de la Renta ceremony dress that blew everyone away. From the Gucci Flora themed bar, to the ceramic lemon detailed plates, this wedding was our top favorite from 2022.
